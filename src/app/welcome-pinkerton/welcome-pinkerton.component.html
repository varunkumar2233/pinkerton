Welcome to the
Pinkerton Risk Portal

The Pinkerton Risk Portal is your one-stop-shop for sampling, purchasing, and viewing Pinkerton Crime Index Reports.

The Pinkerton Crime Index Report is available in two formats:

  1. Standard: A premium offering at a standard price, our Standard Report is perfect for the average client looking for an above-average crime report.
  2. Platinum: Featuring a deeper level of analysis, our Platinum Report is best for advanced clients that demand a little more out of their crime forecasting and analysis.

Pinkerton Crime Index
Report Samples

Preview before you buy with our Pinkerton Crime Index Report Samples.

Featuring popular landmark locals from across the U.S., U.K., and MX, choose from our list of free standard and platinum reports to preview exactly what you’ll receive once you’re ready to purchase a report for a location of your choosing.

Purchasing a Report

Once you’ve selected a report location on the map, we offer two convenient purchase methods:

  1. A la carte: For single report purchases, simply select "add to cart" and continue to billing and payment options.
  2. Credits: When purchasing multiple reports, our credits option offers a convenient way to receive bulk discounts, allowing you to select your desired volume of credits and preview the discount before purchasing. Credits can then be used to redeem reports at your convenience.

Viewing Your Pinkerton
Crime Index Report

Once you’ve completed your purchase, any available reports will be accessible for viewing and download from the “My Reports” section of the homepage.
